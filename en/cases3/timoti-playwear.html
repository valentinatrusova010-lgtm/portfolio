Timoti Playwear

Identity for a children's clothing brand.

1 / 8

About the brand

Timoti Playwear is a children's clothing brand for active play. The clothes are fun, bright and comfortable: children can play in them both at home and outdoors.

Task

Create a recognisable identity for a children's clothing brand that conveys freedom of movement, play and genuine childhood energy.

Solution

The brand identity conveys this playful mood through simple fonts, contrasting colours and a mascot named Timoti — a little friend who accompanies children on their adventures.
