Dandelion Wine

Coffee shop identity.

1 / 13

About the brand

Dandelion Wine is a coffee shop inspired by the novel Dandelion Wine: a place filled with calm, warmth, books and life's simple pleasures.

Task

Create an identity that conveys the atmosphere of the novel and forms a recognisable image for the brand.

Solution

The identity combines warm colours, soft fonts and a dandelion graphic element that supports brand recognition and reinforces its cosy character.
